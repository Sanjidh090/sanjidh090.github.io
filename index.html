Research & Publications

Open data and applied research on Bengali low-resource speech and NLP.

📄 arXiv · 2602.23070 · Feb 2026
Make It Hard to Hear, Easy to Learn: Long-Form Bengali ASR and Speaker Diarization via Extreme Augmentation and Perfect Alignment
Sanjid Hasan · Risalat Labib · A H M Fuad · Bayazid Hasan

Introduces Lipi-Ghor-882 — an 882-hour multi-speaker Bengali speech corpus — and demonstrates that targeted fine-tuning with synthetic acoustic degradation outperforms raw data scaling for low-resource ASR. Won 2nd Runner-Up + Best Dataset Award at DL Sprint 4.0, BUET CSE Fest 2026.
